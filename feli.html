Design Process

1.Problem Statement

"Ineffective writing can hinder communication and limit the impact of your ideas. Many individuals struggle to craft clear, persuasive, and authentic sentences, which can impede their ability to convey their thoughts effectively. Without a solution, the gap between their writing skills and their communication goals remains unbridged. The challenge is to find a tool that can empower writers to enhance their abilities and communicate more persuasively, all while maintaining authenticity."

2.Background Research

" In the landscape of AI text generation platforms, IBM Watson Natural Language Understanding stands out for its comprehensive suite of NLP features, including sentiment analysis and entity recognition, coupled with seamless integration with other IBM Watson services and a user-friendly graphical interface catering to non-technical users. However, it may lag behind some competitors in terms of versatility for content generation tasks. OpenAI's GPT-3 shines with its unparalleled versatility in generating diverse content types and understanding human-like language, powered by its vast pre-trained model. Yet, accessibility comes at a cost, and concerns persist regarding potential biases and limited control over generated content. ChatGPT, a variant of GPT-3 optimized for conversational AI, excels in providing detailed responses in natural language through the OpenAI API but shares similar limitations in content control and potential bias. Meanwhile, Hugging Face Transformers offers a range of pre-trained models and customization options, albeit requiring technical expertise for effective utilization and exhibiting variable performance across tasks. These platforms present diverse options, each with its own set of advantages and drawbacks, catering to different user needs and preferences in AI text generation."

3.Findings

"OpenAI's GPT-3 impressed users with its powerful language generation abilities, finding applications in chatbots and content creation. OpenAI continued refining GPT-3 and exploring new use cases through partnerships. Google BERT improved search engine understanding, leading to more relevant results and advancements in NLP. Google kept refining BERT for various language tasks. ChatGPT was praised for its conversational skills but faced scrutiny for potential biases. It remained useful for chatbots and virtual assistants. Hugging Face's Transformers library gained popularity for its pre-trained models, useful for NLP tasks, with an expanding community. IBM Watson NLU offered NLP capabilities like sentiment analysis, mainly used in business applications. IBM continued refining and integrating Watson services into sectors like healthcare and finance."
