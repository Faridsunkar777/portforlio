Design Process

Discover

Problem Statement

Corporate websites often fail to effectively communicate a company's value proposition and services. Many businesses struggle with outdated designs, poor user experience, and lack of clear information architecture. The challenge is to create a modern, professional website that showcases IFCL's family of subsidiaries and innovative solutions while providing an intuitive user experience.

Research

Background Research

I analyzed existing corporate websites and examined their design patterns, information architecture, and user experience. The research focused on understanding how successful corporate websites present their services, communicate their brand values, and engage their target audience.

Define

Findings

Research revealed that successful corporate websites prioritize clear navigation, compelling storytelling, and visual hierarchy. Users expect easy access to information about services, company background, and contact details. The findings highlighted the importance of a modern design that reflects the company's professionalism and innovation.

Develop

LO-FI Wireframes

Low-fidelity wireframes were created to visualize the website structure and user flow. These wireframes helped define the layout of key pages, navigation structure, and content organization.

Reflection

Reflection

Through an iterative design process, I developed a modern corporate website for IFCL that effectively communicates the company's value proposition and showcases its family of subsidiaries. The website features a clean design, intuitive navigation, and compelling content that engages users and reflects the company's professionalism and innovation.
