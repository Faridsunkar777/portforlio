MONOS

Design Process

Discover

Problem Statement

Many people struggle to learn about Islamic faith and culture due to fragmented resources and lack of structured guidance. There's a need for a comprehensive platform that offers structured learning paths, interactive content, and accessible support to help users explore Islamic teachings, culture, and community.

Research

Background Research

I analyzed existing Islamic learning platforms and resources, examining their features, teaching methods, and user experiences. The research focused on understanding the learning needs of users and identifying gaps in current educational resources.

Define

Findings

Research revealed that users need structured learning paths, interactive content, and accessible information about Islamic faith and culture. Many existing resources lack personalization and fail to provide comprehensive information. The findings highlighted the importance of a user-friendly platform that makes learning about Islamic culture engaging and accessible.

Develop

LO-FI Wireframes

Low-fidelity wireframes were created to visualize the app structure and user flow. These wireframes helped define the layout of key screens, navigation structure, and content organization.

Deliver

HI-FI Wireframes

High-fidelity were created after user testing the lofi wireframes. Users loved the clear navigation and the easy to use interface. Making the new improved app more visually appealing and user-friendly.

Reflection

Reflection

Through an iterative design process, I developed MONOS, a comprehensive Islamic hub that helps users learn about Islamic faith and culture. The app features structured learning paths, interactive content, and accessible information that makes exploring Islamic teachings engaging and user-friendly.
