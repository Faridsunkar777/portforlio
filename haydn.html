Design Process

Step 01

Problem Statement

Ineffective writing can hinder communication and limit the impact of ideas. Many individuals struggle to craft clear, persuasive, and authentic sentences, which impedes their ability to convey thoughts effectively. The challenge is to find a tool that empowers writers to enhance their abilities and communicate more persuasively while maintaining authenticity.

Step 02

Background Research

I analyzed AI text generation platforms including IBM Watson NLU, OpenAI's GPT-3, ChatGPT, and Hugging Face Transformers. Each platform offers unique advantages: IBM Watson excels in NLP features and integration, GPT-3 in versatility, ChatGPT in conversational AI, and Hugging Face in customization. However, each has limitations in content control, bias concerns, or technical complexity.

Step 03

Findings

GPT-3 impressed users with powerful language generation for chatbots and content creation. ChatGPT excelled in conversational skills despite bias concerns. Hugging Face's Transformers gained popularity for NLP tasks with an expanding community. IBM Watson NLU offered sentiment analysis capabilities for business applications. These insights informed the development of a collaborative AI solution.

Step 04

LO-FI Wireframes

Low-fidelity wireframes were created to visualize the user interface and workflow for the AI assistant platform. These initial sketches helped map out the user journey and identify key interaction points for collaborative research and decision-making features.

Conclusion

Reflection

Through an iterative design process informed by advancements in large language models, I developed a collaborative work solution centered around a real-time AI assistant named Haydn. This innovative platform facilitates concurrent research and decision-making within teams, streamlining workflows and accelerating project completion.
