Design Process

Step 01

Problem Statement

Food enthusiasts need a user-centric digital tool that delivers personalized culinary experiences, catering to their discerning taste buds, and simultaneously ensuring affordability. The current challenge lies in the absence of a comprehensive and intuitive user experience platform that seamlessly combines the exploration of exquisite flavors with budget-conscious dining.

Step 02

Background Research

To inform the development of a new restaurant reservation platform, I conducted research on five existing options: OpenTable, Zomato, Yelp, Tasting Table, and Resy. The research focused on understanding their features, strengths, and weaknesses to identify opportunities for improvement.

Step 03

Findings

While some platforms excel in user-friendliness (OpenTable) or detailed reviews (Yelp), they may lack a focus on gourmet dining. Others, like Tasting Table, cater to this niche but have limited geographic reach. The new platform would bridge these gaps by offering a user-friendly interface, a curated selection of high-end restaurants, in-depth information, and reliable user reviews alongside expert recommendations.

Step 04

LO-FI Wireframes

Low-fidelity wireframes were created to visualize the app structure and user flow. These wireframes helped define the layout of key screens, navigation structure, and content organization for the restaurant reservation platform.

Conclusion

Reflection

Users have identified a prevalent issue with many dining establishments being overly expensive, creating a barrier for those seeking exceptional culinary experiences. In response to this need, Diego's Restaurant emerges as the solution that bridges the gap between affordability and quality, offering a unique fine dining experience. Through its intuitive interface, transparent pricing, and personalized recommendations, Diego's Restaurant is set to transform the dining landscape, ensuring that every user can savor exquisite flavors without financial strain.
